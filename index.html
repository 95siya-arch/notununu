About the Firm

Rooted in Mthatha,
Built on Trust

Notununu & Associates has been a cornerstone of the Mthatha legal community for over three decades. Founded on principles of integrity, thoroughness, and genuine client care, the firm has guided countless individuals and businesses through complex legal challenges with confidence and dignity.

Based in Norwood, Mthatha — the heart of the Eastern Cape — our attorneys bring deep regional knowledge and national legal expertise together to provide outstanding representation across a broad spectrum of practice areas.
